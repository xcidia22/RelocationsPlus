Our Mission

RelocationsPlus recognises that relocation is a uniquely people-centric business, hence for every client’s requirements, customisation is offered. Over 20 years, RelocationsPlus has taken the position of an independent quality service provider. It has worked hard to earn trust and has built strong relationships with clients and other service providers. We don’t desire to be the biggest relocation company but we do wish to be recognised as the best, professional, individualised and reliable service provider in Singapore.


To ensure that our services are customised to meet each client’s wishes, we take into consideration the following: • The client company’s policies, budget and objectives. • The specific nature and conditions of each assignment. • The particular and individual needs of the employee.

Hi I'm Sven,

Lliving an expat life is what my family and I have been doing for 15 years now. It doesn’t really seem that long, but that’s what happens when one is enjoying life. Our family is a constellation of East and West, my wife is from Japan and I’m from Germany, so our son is a true global citizen!


My expat adventure began in the United States, followed by a stint in the People’s Republic of China and now as a permanent resident in Singapore, a place we call home. Moving from one country to another has given us an invaluable opportunity to experience life as locals in the new country. But before we were able to appreciate the new environment, we (like many expats) have experienced the endless and unimaginable challenges which came with the new culture: discomfort with the strangeness of it all. It was a series of adjustments to be made not just in the way of life but with our mind-sets. Furthermore, learning and relearning how to deal with different situations, people and everything in general became a daily exercise.


As former head of global change management in Sales for a multinational company, I am fully aware that change, such as relocation can well turn out to be one of the most stressful events in life. I also realised that success in the relocation process can directly influence job performance. Fortunately, I had the advantage of having a knowledgeable, emotionally intelligent and understanding partner. The saying: “a problem shared is a problem halved” is definitely fitting in the relocation process since the family needs every bit of support in dealing with the upheaval during the period of tremendous change.


I have gone through this change process many times as a single person at first, then with a wife and now with a young son and have learned tremendously from people who took care of me and my family during this time. I would like to give this back to you for you to enjoy this wonderful city and all it has to offer from the very beginning. Experience it for yourself and become a lifelong friend and partner of RelocationsPlus.



Why Choose Us?

Hi there! I'm Nancy. My experience in relocation stems from various international assignments which include New York, USA, the Philippines, and Germany and of course Singapore. For over 15 years in Germany, I have assisted many English-speaking families to get adjusted in their new and strange environment.


Having lived in Singapore for more than 20 years, I’d like to help you make the transition into your new environment as easy and pleasurable as possible. Let me tell you a little about who I am and how I can help you. With cross-continent experience and a degree in psychology, I feel well equipped to deal with the multitude of situations which arise when traversing cross-cultural boundaries. .


In April 1998 we moved “lock, stock and barrel” to Singapore. Our two children, then aged 11 and 12, left their school and a host of friends behind: Always a tough circumstance to cope with. But we overcame the transition quickly using our experience to manage the situation. We found a new and rich social scene and swiftly mastered the new way of life. We are neither the first nor the last to have done this sort of move – but having moved from the Philippines to the USA and Europe and then back to Asia, I fully appreciate the overwhelming cultural differences.


These cross-continent moves have given me invaluable insight into how enriching or traumatic relocating can be. The upset and stress these differences often create can be minimised with a little aid from someone who has “been there”. Through my experience I would like to help you to settle easily and comfortably into your new environment in Singapore. In fact to make this your new "home".
